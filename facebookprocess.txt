Process of Two-Factor Authentication on Facebook
Step 1: Login to your Facebook Account and Go to Setting 
Step 2: Click on Security and Login
Step 3: Click on Use two-factor authentication, select a security method you want to implement (In my case I will choose Text Message (SMS))
Step 4: Add your phone number and click continue
Step 5: After Adding your Phone number a code will be send to your given phone number, Enter the correct code that is send on your Phone number
Step 6: After the code is enter correctly it verify the code and the two-factor authentication of Text Message (SMS) is activated
