Process of Two-Factor Authentication on Google
Step 1: Go to myaccount.google.com and login your account and go to security
Step 2: Click on 2-Step Verification
Step 3: Choose the Security method you want to implement (In my case I will choose Text message or voice over call)
Step 4: Add your phone number and choose the option of how do you want to get codes click next
Step 5: After Adding your Phone number a code will be send to your given phone number, Enter the correct code that is send on your Phone number
Step 6: After the code is enter correctly the google verify the code and ask to turn on 2-Step Verification, after clicking on Turn ON the 2-step verification is turn on
